📖 Our Story

Lonoy Agriventures Corporation is a family-owned agribusiness with a legacy spanning over 47 years. We operate an organic farm, a thriving fish pond, and a TESDA-accredited training center in Barangay Lonoy, Citio Asag, Sapian, Capiz.

We are committed to organic farming, environmental sustainability, community employment, and education through our TESDA programs in Organic Agriculture Production NC II.

🎓 TESDA-Accredited Training

We are proud to be a TESDA-accredited training institution offering Organic Agriculture Production NC II.

  • ✅ Hands-on training in organic farming techniques
  • ✅ Sustainable agriculture practices
  • ✅ Community-based learning and employment opportunities
  • ✅ NC II certification upon completion

📍 Training center located at Lonoy Ponds Aqua Farm, Barangay Lonoy, Citio Asag, Sapian, Capiz.
